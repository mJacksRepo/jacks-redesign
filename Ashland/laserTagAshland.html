Laser Invasion

Come to Malibu Jack’s for the most immersive laser tag experience in Ashland. Play individually and tag the most players for the highest score, or huddle up with your team and make a plan. Get creative, climb over obstacles, dodge competitors, and reach bonus objectives to earn more points and avoid getting tagged.

Laser tag makes the perfect Ashland birthday party. They’ll enjoy running around and competing against their friends. Once the kids are done on laser tag, they can head over to go karts, bowling, or even try mini golf. Then they can finish up with virtual reality and arcade games. Laser tag is a great challenge for all ages.

REQUIREMENTS:

Must be 12 years old or older to participate solo. Age 5-11 may participate when accompanied by a paying adult.
