Birthday FAQs

Can I bring a cake?

When you book a party package or rent a party space you are allowed to bring a cake, but not ice cream. No other food or drink is allowed.

Can I bring an ice cream cake?

We encourage you not to bring an ice cream cake. We do allow them, but there is no place to keep them cold at our facility.

ATTRACTION FAQs

Do I need to pay for guests that don't show?

After your 8 participant minimum, you only need to pay for each additional participant that shows. Guests who will not be participating on the attractions are not participants and do not need to pay the participant price.

Can I bring balloons?

YES! Balloons are welcome!

Do we have to sign a waiver for go karts?

No waiver needs to be signed for any attraction at Malibu Jack's.

How old do you have to be do laser tag?

Recommended age is 7 and older. A paid parent or guardian MUST accompany any groups of children age 5-11.

How long does my party get the party space?

The party space is yours for 90 minutes. This time can be spent eating, playing games, celebrating with cake, and visiting with guests. When your 90 minutes is complete, everyone and everything must be out of the party area so we can prepare for the next group. You're welcome to continue playing in the facility.

Do I need to put down a deposit? Does it go towards the cost of the party?

A $53 deposit is required to hold your booking. This is non-refundable. The deposit is applied to the cost of your party.

Do I need to bring plates and forks?

We provide cake plates and forks for all guests, including parents not playing or included in the count.

Can I bring decorations or goodie bags?

You may bring table decorations for your party. We do not allow any party decorations on the walls or cabana posts. We do not allow any confetti in our facility. You may bring goodie bags. We provide them for $3.50 each if you would like to add them to your package.

When should we arrive for our party?

We recommend that you arrive 10-15 minutes prior to your party start time. This will allow us to confirm package details and give you time to order food and drinks for your group (from one of the restaurants at Malibu Jack's).

How long can I expect my birthday party to last?

It depends on what your party package includes but the 'Fun in the Sun' pack usually averages 1-1/2 hours to 2 hours. The 'Ultimate (Full Sail)' package averages 2-3 hours.

Does the birthday person count as a participant in the minimum 8 count?

Yes. The birthday person counts as one of the 8 minimum participants.

Do you charge for any guests who does not want to play?

No, we only charge for guests that would like to participate in any attractions or arcade games.

What is the minimum and maximum number of guests?

The minimum you pay for is 8 guests. There is no maximum. If you have over 25 guests, we prefer for you to speak with the Party Director (Ashland@malibujacks.net).

Do all guests have to do the same attraction together?

No. Each participant has one attraction ticket on their card. They are welcome to use it on any attraction on the Attraction List as long as they meet the attraction age/height requirements.

What about guests that are 3 and under?

Guests that are 3 and under are not able to participate in some of our attractions. Bounce Beach is our primary attraction for kid's under age 10. It is a 9,000 sq ft play space just for kids. This area also contains a Toddler Zone for the younger children. Socks are required in Bounce Beach. An adult must accompany the child and stay in the Bounce Beach area throughout their play time.
