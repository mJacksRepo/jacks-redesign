OTHER HELPFUL INFORMATION

Book your party online today or call the Malibu Jack’s event coordinator at 606-547-4800 or e-mail ashland@malibujacks.net

$53 deposit non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking. At least one adult must stay on premises during parties of children under the age of 15.

No outside food or drink in the facility, including party areas. The only exception is a cake when you book a party package or rent a party space.

Decorations: Balloons and table decorations are welcome! Nothing may be hung on walls, planters, or cabana posts. No glitter, confetti or silly string. Thanks for working with us! If you have more specific questions, please contact a party representative.

Due to limited space and high demand, please be sure to read our cancellation policy.

Cancellation Policy: If a party is cancelled for any reason, no cash refunds will be issued. The $53 deposit is non-refundable.

We also host the following events: Sports Banquets, Holiday Parties, Team Building, Sales Meetings, Employee Appreciation, Corporate Challenges, Client Promotions, Graduations, Lock-Ins, and much more!
