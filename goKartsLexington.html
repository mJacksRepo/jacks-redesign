LEXINGTON

Malibu Speedway

The best go karts experience in Lexington is at Malibu Jack’s, where friends, birthday parties and families can race each other for bragging rights at the Malibu Speedway.

Malibu Jack’s attracts a wide range of visitors because the indoor theme park brings so many different attractions inside the same building. If you think of go karts as gas-powered, loud and outdoors, think again. Malibu Jack’s uses modern, electric go karts that offer instant power, nimble handling, and fun speeds. And every driver gets three speed boosts per race.

You can choose between two types of go karts. The one-seater karts are for solo drivers who like to race on their own. Drivers of these karts must be at least 10 years old and 56” tall. The two-seater karts allow an adult driver to bring along a passenger who is age 4-17 and 38” tall. And the passenger rides free!

SOther go kart facilities are more expensive and either only for adult drivers or outside in the elements. Not at Malibu Jack’s. Whether the weather in Lexington is cold, rainy, snowy, hot or humid, it’s always perfect for go karts inside Malibu Jack’s. Each race can accommodate up to 8 racers so you can beat all of your friends at once.

Safety is always our highest priority, and each race is run by a qualified marshal who can stop karts as needed.
