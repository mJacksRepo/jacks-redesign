Jack's Arcade

For the biggest and best collection of arcade games in Ashland, all ages enjoy Malibu Jack’s. We have over 10,000 square feet to test your skills, from racing to shooting to puzzles and more. Earn prizes and compete with your friends!

If you enjoy the nostalgia of classic arcade games, we’ve got you covered on our boardwalk with vintage options. And play twice as long on Wednesday with all games half-price. You can also play the most popular new games, earn redemption points, and cash those in for prizes large and small.

Don’t miss your chance to immerse into a different world through our virtual reality experiences. Choose between the XRider 4D motion simulator, the Dark Ride 4D motion theater with blasters, or our VR Hologate with virtual reality headsets and team-based battles.

You can also test your jump shot at the All-Star Basketball Shootout. Race against the clock to score as many points as you can, and receive a prize based on your accuracy and how you fared with the bonus money balls. Ashland arcade lovers keep coming back to Malibu Jack’s to set new high scores and rack up rewards.
