Clean & Safe Fun

Malibu Jack's Responsibilities

Our goal is clean and safe fun for everyone. We have taken some precautions to ensure a clean, safe, and fun visit to Malibu Jack's:

REDUCED OCCUPANCY:

Maximum occupancies will be limited to 60% of rated capacity. Individual attractions may have reduced occupancy and will be posted at each attraction to ensure compliance.

DESIGNATED ENTRANCES AND EXITS

Malibu Jack's will have designated doors for entering and exiting the building. Signage upon entry will highlight Malibu Jack's guidelines to ensure the fun and safety of our guests. We ask that all guests uphold our guidelines so that we can stay safe together.

MASKS REQUIRED

All Malibu Jack's team members are required to wear masks unless they are vaccinated. Guests age 12 and older must wear masks unless they are vaccinated. Guests age 6-11 are required to wear masks.

SANITIZATION STATIONS

Malibu Jack's will feature sanitation stations on the main walkways throughout each facility. The stations are for guests and team members to use as needed. Guests are recommended to sanitize hands before and after using any game or attraction.

TOUCH POINTS

High-contact surfaces throughout Malibu Jack's will be wiped down with a sanitizing agent. Malibu Jack's will have a dedicated sanitization team focused on these high-contact surfaces.

ANTIVIRAL AIR FILTER

Nu-Calgon air purification system that has been tested to treat against viruses and bacteria to better protect guests and employees. Kills mold, bacteria, and viruses. Reduces allergens, odors and smoke. *Louisville location only.

TEMPERATURE CHECKS

All Malibu Jack's team members will be required to have their temperature taken daily upon check-in. If a team member’s temperature is 100.4℉ or higher (per CDC guideline), the team member will be sent home until they are fever or symptom free.

SOCIAL DISTANCING

All team members and guests must practice 6’ social distancing as recommended by the CDC and local health authorities. To assist with social distancing measures, signage throughout our facilities will promote the recommended 6 feet of spacing.

VISIBLE SIGNAGE

Malibu Jack's signage will be strategically placed throughout our facilities to communicate standards in place. Signage will be evident throughout the venue

Guest Responsibilities

Our goal is clean and safe fun for everyone. We kindly ask our guests to follow a few basic responsibilities to ensure a clean, safe, and fun visit to Malibu Jack's:

STAY HOME WHEN SICK

Please stay home if you or a family member is not feeling well. We are excited for you to return when you’re feeling better.

MASKS REQUIRED

Guests age 12 and older must wear masks unless they are vaccinated. Guests age 6-11 are required to wear masks.

PERSONAL HABITS

Do your best to avoid touching your face while at Malibu Jack's. Kindly cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.

KEEP HANDS CLEAN

Please wash your hands periodically and properly while at Malibu Jack’s. Guests are recommended to sanitize hands before and after using any game or attraction.

SOCIAL DISTANCING

Adhere to social distancing guidelines. While floor markers will denote where to stand in queue lines, uphold the recommended 6 feet at all times. Families are encouraged to stay together in queues and throughout their visit.
