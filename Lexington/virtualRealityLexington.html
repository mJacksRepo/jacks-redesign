Virtual Reality

Explore new worlds at Malibu Jack's! Virtual reality experiences include: Dark Ride, XRider, and Hologate VR.

Dark Ride 4D motion theater is an attraction for up to 8 players that takes you on crazy adventures! Blast your way to victory as you compete with other players for the high score!

The XRider is an 8 person roller coaster simulator. Choose from a variety of movies to experience!

Enter another world and fight off waves of robots in Hologate VR. This team based VR experience is for up to four players. Battle AI Robots and compete for the high score!

REQUIREMENTS:

DARK RIDE

Must be 38" or taller to ride.

Max weight 350 lbs.

X-RIDER

Rider: Must be 48" or taller.

Passenger: Must be 38" to 47" tall to ride with paying adult (age 18 or older).

Passenger must be between 38" to 47" to ride with paying adult.

HOLOGATE VR

Must be age 7 or older AND 45" or taller.
