LEXINGTON

Laser Invasion

Come to Malibu Jack’s for the most immersive laser tag experience in Lexington. Play individually and tag the most players for the highest score, or huddle up with your team and make a plan. Get creative, climb over obstacles, dodge competitors, and reach bonus objectives to earn more points and avoid getting tagged.

Laser tag makes the perfect Lexington birthday party. They’ll enjoy running around and competing against their friends. Enjoy a reserved party cabana, party host, and food from Jack’s Sandbar and Grill. Once the kids are done on laser tag, they can head over to go karts, bowling, or even try mini golf. Then they can finish up with virtual reality and arcade games. Laser tag is a great challenge for all ages. Come compete with your friends during a night out, and then you can head over to Jack’s Sandbar and Grill.

REQUIREMENTS:

Must be 12 years old or older to participate solo. Age 5-11 may participate when accompanied by a paying adult.
