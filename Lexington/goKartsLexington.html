LEXINGTON

Malibu Speedway

The best go karts experience in Lexington is at Malibu Jack’s, where friends, birthday parties and families can race each other for bragging rights at the Malibu Speedway.

You can choose between two types of go karts.

Each race can accommodate up to 8 racers so you can beat all of your friends at once.

Safety is always our highest priority, and each race is run by a qualified marshal who can stop karts as needed.

REQUIREMENTS:

SOLO DRIVER

Must be 10 years old or older. Must be 56" or taller. Max weight is 300 lbs.

DRIVER + PASSENGER

Driver must be age 18 or older AND 56" or taller. Passenger must be between ages 4-17 AND 38" or taller. Max combined weight is 550 lbs. Passenger is FREE with paying driver.
