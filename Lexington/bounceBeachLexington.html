Bounce Beach Playground

At Malibu Jack's your young ones find their oasis: 12,000 square feet and three levels of the most fun indoor playground for toddlers in Lexington. Whether they love slides, or climbing, or just running around through the playground this is the place for kids.

Guardians are free to follow their kids around or have a seat on a relaxing chair as kids have fun with good friends and new ones too.

REQUIREMENTS:

Age 10 and younger only. Toddler area for ages 3 and younger only. Socks required. Children must be accompanied by an adult at all times. Adults not allowed in play structures.
