Boardwalk Bowling

Whether celebrating a birthday party with your kids or enjoying a night with friends, bowling at Malibu Jack’s Louisville is the perfect experience for all ages. Our bowling lanes can be customized for beginners or experienced players. And the state-of-the-art electronic scoring system keeps track of all the friendly competition. Book your bowling lane for your next event in Louisville, or at one of our locations in your area.

Malibu Jack’s offers the best options for birthday parties in Louisville. Our staff is standing by to answer your questions and improve your experience. We can help you pick the right ball, learn where to aim and even to bowl with your kids. Bumpers are available to stay out of the gutters, and we also have ramps to guide kids’ shots down the lane.
