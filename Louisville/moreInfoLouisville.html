Attractions

Age/Height Requirements

ARCADE

PRICING:

$0.50 - $3.00

REQUIREMENTS:

There's an arcade game for everyone from toddlers to adults.

BOWLING

PRICING:

Mon thru Thurs: $4 game + $3 shoe rental

Fri thru Sun: $25/hr per lane + $3 shoe rental

REQUIREMENTS:

Everyone can enjoy this attraction. No reservations. Lanes are first come, first serve.

GO KARTS

PRICING:

$8 per driver. *Passengers FREE

REQUIREMENTS:

One Passenger: Must be 10 years old or older. Must be 56" or taller. Max weight is 300 lbs.


Two Passengers: Driver must be age 18 or older AND 56" or taller. Passenger must be between ages 4-17 and 38" or taller. Max combined weight is 550 lbs. Passenger is FREE with paying driver.

SPIN ZONE

PRICING:

$5 per driver. *Passengers FREE

REQUIREMENTS:

Driver: Must be 5 years old or older AND 38" or taller. Must be 18 years old or older to drive a passenger.


Optional Passenger: Must be 3 years old or older AND 36" or taller. Passenger is free with paying driver.

LASER TAG

PRICING:

$8 per participant

REQUIREMENTS:

Must be 12 years old or older to participate solo. Age 5-11 may participate when accompanied by a paying adult.

BOUNCE BEACH

PRICING:

$10 for day pass

REQUIREMENTS:

Age 10 and younger only. Toddler area for ages 3 and younger only. Socks required. Children must be accompanied by an adult at all times. Adults not allowed in play structures.

MALIBU GOLF

PRICING:

$8 per golfer. Kids age 4 and under play free

REQUIREMENTS:

Children under age 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Kids age 4 and under play free.

DARK RIDE

PRICING:

$6 per rider

REQUIREMENTS:

Must be 38" or taller to ride. Max weight 350 lbs.

HOLOGATE VR

PRICING:

$7 per participant

REQUIREMENTS:

Must be 7 years old or older and 45" or taller.

X RIDER

PRICING:

$5 per rider

REQUIREMENTS:

Rider: Must be 48" or taller.

Passenger: Guests between 38" to 47" tall to ride with paying adult.

TWIST N SHOUT

PRICING:

$5 per rider

REQUIREMENTS:

Rider: Must be 47" or taller. Max height 6'5". Max weight 240 lbs.

Passenger: Must be 4 years old or older and 38" tall to ride with paying adult.

WAVE RIDER

PRICING:

$5 per participant

REQUIREMENTS:

Rider: Must be age 10 or older and 55" or taller.

Passenger: Must be 8 or older and 51" or taller to ride with a rider.

SWINGING SHIP

PRICING:

$4 per rider

REQUIREMENTS:

Rider: Must be 47" or taller. Max height 6'5". Max weight 240 lbs.

Passenger: Must be 3 years old or older and 36" tall to ride with paying adult.
