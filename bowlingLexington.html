Boardwalk Bowling

Whether you are getting together with old friends or looking for your next fun family activity, bowling is a great game to bring people together. At Boardwalk Bowling in Lexington we have lanes that cater to pros and first timers alike at the best price in town.

Located next to our Jack’s Bar and Grill, Boardwalk Bowling feels like a party with our high powered sound systems and LED light system. Want to catch up with friends but don’t want to miss the big game? This is the place as we have high resolution projector screens able to play your favorite sports on demand.

Our bowling lanes are designed with all players in mind as kids 2+ can enjoy bowling with state of the art bumpers that rise automatically for the younger bowlers. We also have ramps for those who would like to play but have difficulty rolling the ball.
